XR Research

VR Liver Surgery Simulation

This project explores how Unity and the SOFA Framework can be used for soft-body medical simulation, surgical tool interaction, and immersive training environments.

Role
Unity/XR development and simulation exploration
Stack
Unity, C#, SOFA Framework, VR interaction
Focus
Soft-body deformation, collision handling, low-latency interaction

What I am building

The goal is to make a medical training simulation where soft tissue can react to tools in a realistic way. I am exploring mesh deformation, physics-based collision, tool control, and how to keep the experience responsive enough for VR.

Learning areas

  • Unity scene setup and VR interaction patterns.
  • Soft-body simulation using the SOFA Framework.
  • Mesh deformation and collision response.
  • Performance tuning for low-latency immersive experiences.
