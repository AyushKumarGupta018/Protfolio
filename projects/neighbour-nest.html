Community Commerce App

Neighbour Nest

Neighbour Nest is a production-style Flutter and Firebase app for connecting people with nearby stores, clinics, and local service providers.

Role
App architecture, UI, Firebase integration
Stack
Flutter, Firebase Auth, Firestore, Storage, notifications
Focus
Local commerce, appointments, orders, multilingual access

Why I built it

I wanted to build something useful for a real community setting, not only a demo app. The idea was to make local services easier to discover and use, especially when people need quick access to stores, clinics, emergency help, or neighborhood support.

Features

  • OTP-based authentication and account flows.
  • Real-time Firestore listings for stores, clinics, and providers.
  • Appointment booking, order placement, and notifications.
  • Multilingual support for wider accessibility.
  • Mobile-first interface designed for repeated everyday use.
