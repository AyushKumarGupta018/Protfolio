AI Product

Fortress AI

Fortress AI is an AI/security product concept where I explored how monitoring, intelligence, and decision-support workflows can be shaped into a usable interface.

Role
Product thinking, workflow design, prototype direction
Stack
AI workflows, security dashboard concepts, app interface planning
Focus
Defense-style workflows, alerts, situational awareness

What I explored

This project helped me think beyond a single model or screen. I treated it like a product system: what should the user see first, what should be treated as urgent, how should alerts be organized, and how can AI support decisions without making the interface confusing?

Project direction

  • AI-assisted monitoring and alert flows.
  • Dashboard-style information architecture.
  • Security-focused workflows and prioritization.
  • Interfaces designed for quick scanning and action.
