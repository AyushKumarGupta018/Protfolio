Hack N Tech Winner

AI Smart Helmet

I led the mobile side of an AI-powered smart helmet project built to detect traffic signals and alert riders in real time.

Role
Mobile app lead
Stack
Flutter, YOLOv5/YOLOv8, FastAPI, TFLite exploration
Outcome
Winning project at IIT Patna Hack N Tech

Project idea

The project focused on helping two-wheeler riders notice traffic signals and road warnings faster. My part was building the rider-facing mobile experience and connecting the live camera/detection flow with clear alerts.

What I handled

  • Designed the Flutter interface for rider-focused use.
  • Worked with real-time camera input and alert states.
  • Integrated backend detection flow through FastAPI.
  • Explored TensorFlow Lite for lower-latency on-device inference.
  • Helped the team prepare a working demo under hackathon pressure.
